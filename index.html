Instead of trying to select a group that is representative of the US population, we can simply take our chances and pick people at random from across the country. By “at random,” we mean that any person in the US is as likely to be picked as any other person. A groupof people picked in this way is called a random sample. Due to chance alone, some of the people that we pick will almost certainly turn out to have a higher than average income.
